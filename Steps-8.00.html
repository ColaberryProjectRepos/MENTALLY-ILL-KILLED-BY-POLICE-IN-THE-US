Connecting to data source 8

Step 1. Open Power BI and on the upper left ribbon, click on get data and choose online  data source.

Step 2. Fill out the web URL and load the data. Here "Data LINK" is the data source.

Step 3. Visualize your Data and identify unique key (In this case the ID column) and needed columns.
